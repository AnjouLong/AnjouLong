Piggy Wiggy

Piggy Wiggy is a lady from Jiangxi Agricultural University who majors in English and get into the school in 2016.
She is beautiful, lovly, smart and cute.
She always studies hard on her major courses, and sometimes spares her time on part-time jobs to get little pocket money for her make-ups.
However, though Wiggy most of the times stay gentle as the summer breeze, she can also turn nasty like the storms after a tsunami.

But this does not decline her beauty, and moreover add to her charm.
